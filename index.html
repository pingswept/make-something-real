ME43: Senior Design Project

The Senior Design Project is a capstone class taught by Brandon Stafford in the fall of 2018 at Tufts University. (There is another section taught by Dr. Kristen Wendell.)

The point of the class is to provide you with hands-on experience building a complex mechanical system in a team, to complement the theory that you learned in the rest of your classes. To that end, the class consists of one large project, which changes each year.
